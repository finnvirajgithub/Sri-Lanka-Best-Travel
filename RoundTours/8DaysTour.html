8-Day Journey

Round Tours

"Culture, Nature, Highlands, Safari, Beaches"

Uncover the soul of Sri Lanka on this 8-day journey through sacred cities, misty hills, wildlife sanctuaries, and sun-kissed beaches. From ancient Anuradhapura to scenic Ella and Yala’s wild jungles to Mirissa’s shores, this immersive tour blends history, adventure, and relaxation in one unforgettable island experience.

Tour Itinerary

1Day 01: Welcome to Paradise – Arrival in Colombo & Transfer to Negombo

Begin your island adventure with a warm welcome at Bandaranaike International Airport. From there, it’s a short drive to the coastal town of Negombo. Spend your first day at leisure — stroll along golden beaches, catch a scenic sunset, or unwind at your beachfront hotel. Overnight in Negombo.

Travel Distance : 10 Km

Travel Hour : 1 Hour

Overnight at : Negombo

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

2Day 02: Temples & Ancient Capitals – Negombo to Anuradhapura

Head north into Sri Lanka’s cultural heart. Stop at Munneswaram Temple, a revered Hindu sanctuary. Continue to Anuradhapura, the island’s ancient capital. Explore sacred landmarks like the 2200-year-old Sri Maha Bodhi tree and the majestic Ruwanweliseya stupa. Stay overnight at a tranquil eco-resort surrounded by nature.

Travel Distance : 180 Km

Travel Hour : 6 Hours

Overnight at : Anuradhapura

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

3Day 03: Forest Trails & Village Life – Anuradhapura to Sigiriya

After breakfast, set out toward Sigiriya. Along the way, discover the serene ruins of the Ritigala Forest Monastery. In the afternoon, experience rural hospitality on a cultural village tour, complete with tuk-tuk rides, canoe crossings, and bullock carts. In the evening, climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress for sweeping sunset views. Overnight in Sigiriya.

Travel Distance : 100 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Sigiriya

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

4Day 04: Temples & Traditions – Sigiriya to Kandy

Begin with a visit to Dambulla’s Golden Cave Temple, renowned for its ancient Buddhist art. Next, stop at a fragrant spice garden in Matale. By afternoon, arrive in Kandy, where you’ll witness the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a vibrant cultural dance performance. Stay in a scenic hill-view hotel.

Travel Distance : 100 Km

Travel Hour : 7 Hours

Overnight at : Kandy

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

5Day 05: Tea Country Bliss – Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

Travel to the highlands of Nuwara Eliya, dubbed “Little England” for its colonial charm. Along the way, explore the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens and a local gem museum. Visit a working tea factory and sip authentic Ceylon tea before a walk through Nuwara Eliya’s quaint town center. Overnight in the misty hills.

Travel Distance : 80 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Nuwara Eliya

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

6Day 06: Scenic Rails & Southern Wilds – Nuwara Eliya to Ella to Yala

Catch one of the world’s most breathtaking train rides from Nanu Oya to Ella. Discover Ella’s lush surroundings, including the Nine Arch Bridge and Mini Adam’s Peak. Later, descend toward the southern plains for an overnight stay near Yala National Park. Stay at a nature resort near the wilderness.

Travel Distance : 180 Km

Travel Hour : 10 Hours

Overnight at : Yala

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

7Day 07: Safari & Seaside – Yala to Mirissa

Start your day with an exciting 4x4 safari through Yala National Park, home to leopards, elephants, and crocodiles. Then head to Mirissa, a laid-back beach haven. Spend the afternoon relaxing, or try out optional activities like snorkeling or jet skiing. Watch the sun melt into the ocean at your beach resort.

Travel Distance : 150 Km

Travel Hour : 4 Hours

Overnight at : Mirissa

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

8Day 08: Final Impressions – Mirissa to Colombo

Wrap up your adventure with a drive to Colombo. Enjoy a guided city tour covering heritage buildings, bustling markets, and coastal charm. Pick up souvenirs or enjoy a farewell lunch before your transfer to the airport or hotel. Your unforgettable Sri Lankan journey ends here.

Travel Distance : 170 Km

Travel Hour : 3 Hours

Drop at : Colombo / Airport

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)
