15 - Day Sri Lanka Grand Odyssey

"An unforgettable journey through heritage, wildlife, beaches, and cultural treasures"

Experience the best of Sri Lanka on this 15-day grand odyssey through ancient cities, wildlife safaris, tea plantations, and golden beaches. From cultural wonders to coastal retreats, each day offers unique adventures and unforgettable memories. Perfect for travelers seeking heritage, nature, and relaxation in one captivating island journey.

Tour Itinerary

1Day 1: Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Negombo
Welcome to Sri Lanka! Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB), you’ll be warmly greeted by your personal English-speaking chauffeur. After a brief introduction and assistance, transfer to your hotel in Negombo (approx. 30 minutes). Settle in and relax by the coastal breeze.
2Day 2: Negombo – Pinnawala – Dambulla – Sigiriya
Begin your journey with a scenic drive to Sigiriya. En route, visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage or the Millennium Elephant Foundation to meet rescued elephants. Continue to the magnificent Dambulla Cave Temple, home to ancient Buddha statues and murals. Arrive in Sigiriya and check into your nature resort.
3Day 3: Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Polonnaruwa Ancient Kingdom
Start the day by climbing the legendary Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka’s most iconic landmarks. Later, head to Polonnaruwa for a guided tour through the ruins of this medieval capital. Return to Sigiriya for an overnight stay.
4Day 4: Safari & Village Life – Transfer to Anuradhapura
Embark on an early morning wildlife safari at Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park, where large elephant herds roam freely. Then, enjoy an authentic village safari experience with a bullock cart ride, traditional cooking demonstration, and local lunch. Later, travel to Anuradhapura and check into your hotel.
5Day 5: Ancient Anuradhapura & Nilaveli Beach
After breakfast, explore the sacred city of Anuradhapura, including visits to the sacred Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi tree and the towering Ruwanwelisaya stupa. In the afternoon, head east to Trincomalee, stopping at the pristine Nilaveli Beach before checking into your seaside resort.
6Day 6: Explore Trincomalee’s Coastal Beauty
Spend the day discovering Trincomalee’s charm with visits to Kandaswamy Temple and the beautiful coastal landscapes. Optional activities include scuba diving, snorkeling, or fishing. Enjoy a laid-back afternoon by the sea.
7Day 7: Trincomalee – Pasikudah Beach Escape
After breakfast, take a leisurely drive to the calm shores of Pasikudah, known for its shallow crystal-clear waters. Check-in at your beachfront hotel and enjoy a day of swimming, sunbathing, or simply unwinding.
8Day 8: Pasikudah – Scenic Drive to Kandy
Leave the east coast behind and journey toward the central hills of Kandy. En route, stop at the Matale Spice Garden for a guided tour. Arrive in Kandy, the cultural capital, and attend a lively Kandyan cultural dance performance in the evening. Overnight stay in Kandy.
9Day 9: Sacred Kandy & Hill Country Journey to Nuwara Eliya
Begin the day with a visit to the revered Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa). Afterward, browse gem museums and batik shops before heading to Nuwara Eliya. En route, explore a working tea plantation and factory. Visit Gregory Lake and enjoy the cool mountain air. Overnight in Nuwara Eliya.
10Day 10: Nuwara Eliya – Ella – Yala Safari
Depart for Yala, stopping along the way to visit the famous Nine Arch Bridge and take in the views from Ella Rock. In the afternoon, venture into Yala National Park on a 4x4 jeep safari to spot leopards, elephants, and other wildlife. Check-in at your safari lodge for a relaxing evening.
11Day 11: Yala – Coastal Retreat in Mirissa
After breakfast, travel along the southern coastline to Mirissa. Check in at your beachside hotel and unwind by the shore. Enjoy Mirissa’s vibrant beach vibes and fresh seafood cuisine by night.
12Day 12: Whale Watching & Beach Leisure in Mirissa
Set off early on a whale-watching cruise, one of the best in the region for spotting blue whales and dolphins. Spend the rest of the day enjoying water sports or relaxing at the beach.
13Day 13: Mirissa – Galle Fort Heritage Tour
Travel to the historic city of Galle. Explore the iconic Dutch Fort, walk along the ramparts, visit the lighthouse, and step into charming streets filled with colonial-era architecture. Stay overnight in a boutique hotel within or near the fort.
14Day 14: Galle – Colombo City Experience
Head back to Colombo, Sri Lanka’s bustling commercial capital. Upon arrival, take a guided city tour visiting Galle Face Green, the Gangaramaya Temple, the Independence Memorial Hall, and the National Museum. Cap off your day with shopping and relaxation at your hotel.
15Day 15: Departure from Colombo
Your unforgettable Sri Lankan odyssey comes to an end. After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, carrying cherished memories of a journey through paradise.
