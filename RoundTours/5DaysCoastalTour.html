"Sun, Culture, Wildlife – Coastal Wonder"

Discover Sri Lanka’s coastal elegance on a 5-day journey through beaches, colonial towns, and vibrant marine life. From Colombo’s cultural charm to the shores of Bentota, Galle, and Mirissa, this tour offers an unforgettable blend of history, relaxation, and adventure — ending with a scenic farewell in Negombo.

Tour Itinerary

1Day 01: Touchdown in Colombo – City Rhythms and Cultural Beginnings
Upon your arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you'll be warmly received by our Roam Sri Lanka guide and support team. After a brief drive to the heart of Colombo, dive into the city’s pulse with a guided exploration. Visit the National Museum and the serene Gangaramaya Temple, blending colonial history with spiritual calm. Overnight stay in Colombo.
2Day 02: Coastal Charms – Colombo to Bentota & Galle
After breakfast, journey south along the sun-kissed coastline. Stop at the beautiful Bentota Beach and pay a visit to a local turtle conservation hatchery. Continue to Galle — a coastal gem infused with colonial character. Explore the iconic Galle Fort, stroll the old ramparts, visit the Maritime Museum, and enjoy the view from the lighthouse. Overnight in Galle.
3Day 03: Galle to Mirissa – Tropical Tranquility Awaits
Begin your day with a leisurely drive to the laid-back beach town of Mirissa. En route, stop at Unawatuna Beach to dip your toes in the clear waters. Once in Mirissa, unwind along its palm-fringed shores or simply relax at your beachfront resort. Spend the evening soaking in the coastal calm.
4Day 04: Whale Watching & Southern Coast – Mirissa to Negombo
Start your morning early with an exhilarating whale-watching excursion — Mirissa is one of the best places in the world to witness blue whales in the wild. Afterwards, set off for the charming fishing town of Negombo. In the afternoon, explore the buzzing fish markets, visit the colonial-era Dutch Fort, and walk along the golden Negombo Beach. Overnight in Negombo.
5Day 05: Farewell Sri Lanka – Departure from Negombo
Enjoy a relaxed breakfast before heading to the airport with your chauffeur guide. As you bid farewell to this island paradise, take with you memories of golden beaches, cultural gems, and unforgettable marine encounters.
