12 Days – Sri Lanka Discovery Tour

Home

"Whales, Waterfalls, Temples, Wild Elephants"

Discover Sri Lanka's vibrant soul on this 12-day journey of scenic beauty, cultural treasures, and unforgettable wildlife. From misty highlands and ancient cities to white-sand beaches and thrilling safaris, experience it all—elephant encounters, tea country charm, whale watching, and more. A perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and heritage awaits.

Tour Itinerary

1Day 1: Arrival in Sri Lanka / Airport or Hotel Pick-up
Ayubowan! Welcome to Sri Lanka. Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB), meet your personal tour guide and transfer to your beachfront hotel in Negombo. Take time to relax, feel the ocean breeze, and adjust to the island rhythm before your grand journey begins.
2Day 2: Pinnawala Elephants / Village Life & Sigiriya Fortress
Start your day with a visit to the Pinnawala Elephant Rehabilitation Centre to witness majestic giants up close. Later, enjoy an authentic village experience including a tuk-tuk ride, cooking demonstration, and traditional lunch. In the evening, climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Overnight stay at a nature resort in Sigiriya.
3Day 3: Polonnaruwa Kingdom & Elephant Safari
Cycle through the UNESCO World Heritage ruins of Polonnaruwa, exploring ancient temples and royal palaces. In the afternoon, head out on a 4x4 Jeep Safari in either Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park, home to large herds of wild elephants. End the day with optional Ayurvedic relaxation. Overnight in Sigiriya.
4Day 4: Golden Temple, Spice Garden & Kandy Culture
Explore the Dambulla Golden Cave Temple, renowned for its ancient murals and Buddha statues. Visit a spice garden in Matale and then continue to Kandy. Tour the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a vibrant Kandyan cultural dance performance. Overnight stay at a hillside hotel in Kandy.
5Day 5: Kitulgala Adventure & White Water Rafting
Drive through lush countryside to Kitulgala, the island’s adventure capital. Feel the thrill of white-water rafting on the Kelani River, followed by a short jungle trek to a natural rock pool and waterfall. Unwind in a peaceful nature retreat in Kitulgala or return to Kandy for the night.
6Day 6: Misty Mountains & Tea Plantations
Travel through Sri Lanka’s central highlands to Nuwara Eliya, also known as “Little England.” Visit a working tea plantation and factory for a tasting session. Explore the Royal Botanical Garden in Peradeniya and take a walking tour of Nuwara Eliya’s colonial town. Overnight in a cozy hilltop villa.
7Day 7: Scenic Train Ride & Ella Excursion
Enjoy one of the world’s most scenic train rides from Nanu-Oya to Ella. After arrival, visit the Nine Arch Bridge and hike to Mini Adam’s Peak for panoramic mountain views. Optional visit to Ravana Waterfall. Overnight stay in a charming mountain-view hotel in Ella.
8Day 8: Yala National Park Leopard Safari
Rise early for an optional Ella Rock trek at sunrise. Then journey south to Yala National Park. In the afternoon, go on a thrilling 4x4 game drive to spot leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and exotic birds. Rest and recharge in a safari lodge near the park.
9Day 9: Relax on Mirissa Beach
Drive along the southern coast to the tranquil shores of Mirissa or Dickwella. Spend your day unwinding on golden sands, swimming in warm turquoise waters, or savoring fresh seafood at a beachfront restaurant. Overnight in a serene beach resort.
10Day 10: Whale Watching & Galle Fort
Set out early for a whale watching cruise in the Indian Ocean — a chance to see blue whales and dolphins in their natural habitat. Continue to the historic Galle Dutch Fort, a UNESCO site rich in colonial charm. Overnight stay in a boutique hotel in Galle.
11Day 11: Bentota Beach & Turtle Hatchery
Enjoy the sunshine at Bentota Beach with optional water sports like jet skiing and banana boat rides. Visit a local turtle conservation hatchery and, if you wish, explore the scenic Madu Ganga River on a boat safari. Overnight at a luxury beachfront resort.
12Day 12: Colombo City Tour / Airport Drop-off
Explore Colombo’s bustling streets, colonial architecture, temples, and modern attractions. Visit Independence Square, the Old Parliament, and Galle Face Green. Shop for souvenirs and enjoy your final moments in Sri Lanka before being transferred to the airport or your next destination.
