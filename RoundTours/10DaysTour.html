10 Days Sri Lanka Grand Discovery

Home

Round Tours

"Ancient Cities, Tea, Wildlife, Beaches"

Explore the best of Sri Lanka in 10 unforgettable days. Discover ancient kingdoms, climb iconic rock fortresses, wander lush tea plantations, and go on thrilling safaris. Unwind on pristine beaches and experience vibrant culture, scenic train rides, and warm hospitality. This journey blends history, nature, and relaxation beautifully.

Tour Itinerary

1Day 01: Arrival in Colombo

Ayubowan! Upon arrival at Colombo CMB International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by your tour leader. Your first destination is Negombo, a charming coastal town famous for its golden sandy beaches and vibrant fishing community. As no activities are scheduled for the day, you can unwind at your beachfront hotel, enjoy the sea breeze, or take a peaceful stroll along the shore. Witness the spectacular sunset before settling in for an overnight stay at a cozy seafront hotel in Negombo.

Travel Distance : 10 Km

Travel Hour : 1 Hour

Overnight at : Negombo

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

2Day 02: To Anuradhapura

After a hearty breakfast accompanied by a cup of authentic Ceylon tea, begin your journey to the ancient city of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the first royal capital of Sri Lanka. En route, stop by the Munneshwaram Temple, a revered Hindu pilgrimage site. Upon arrival in Anuradhapura, explore the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi tree—over 2200 years old—and the majestic Ruwanweliseya stupa. Soak in the spiritual aura of this ancient kingdom before retiring to a tranquil nature-friendly resort for the night.

Travel Distance : 180 Km

Travel Hour : 6 Hours

Overnight at : Anuradhapura

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

3Day 03: To Dambulla via Ritigala

On Day 3, set off towards Dambulla, located at the heart of Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle. Weather permitting, visit the mystical Ritigala Forest Monastery nestled within untouched wilderness. Later, enjoy a cultural village tour: take a Tuk-Tuk ride to a local farmer’s home, cross a serene lake by canoe to admire the Sigiriya landscape, and end with a bullock cart ride through the lush countryside. In the evening, climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO site, and marvel at the sunset from the summit. Overnight stay will be at a nature-friendly resort in Dambulla.

Travel Distance : 100 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Dambulla

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

4Day 04: To Kandy

After breakfast, travel to Kandy, the cultural capital and last royal stronghold of the Sinhalese kings. On the way, stop at the Dambulla Royal Cave Temple, which houses over 150 Buddhist statues and intricate murals, and later explore the Matale Spice Garden to learn about Sri Lanka’s fragrant spices. In the afternoon, visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic—a UNESCO World Heritage Site—followed by a vibrant cultural dance show showcasing traditional Sri Lankan arts. Spend the night in a scenic mountain-view hotel in Kandy.

Travel Distance : 80 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Kandy

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

5Day 05: To Nuwara Eliya

Day 5 takes you to Nuwara Eliya, affectionately known as “Little England” for its colonial charm and cool climate. Begin with visits to the Gem Museum and the Royal Botanical Garden in Peradeniya, home to exotic flora. Continue your journey through misty hills and endless tea plantations. Visit a working tea factory to witness the age-old art of making Ceylon tea. End your day with a leisurely walk through Nuwara Eliya town and its bustling local market. Overnight stay at a cozy mount-view villa.

Travel Distance : 80 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Nuwara Eliya

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

6Day 06: Nuwara Eliya - Ella

Enjoy a breathtaking train ride from Nanu-Oya to Ella, regarded as one of the most scenic rail journeys in the world. Arrive in the idyllic hill village of Ella, where you’ll visit the famous Nine Arch Bridge and hike up Mini Adam’s Peak for panoramic views. Later, relax at a local café or explore Ella’s vibrant village life. Your overnight stay will be at a comfortable mountain-view hotel in Ella.

Travel Distance : 65 Km

Travel Hour : 4 Hours

Overnight at : Ella

Transfer Mode : Train

7Day 07: To Yala via Ella Rock

If you're up for an adventure, start your day with a sunrise hike to Ella Rock. Afterwards, continue your journey toward Yala. Pause to admire the cascading Ravana Falls along the way. In the afternoon, set off on an exciting 4x4 jeep safari through Yala National Park, where you may encounter leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and crocodiles in their natural habitat. End your adventurous day with an overnight stay in a safari lodge at Tissamaharama.

Travel Distance : 120 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Yala

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

8Day 08: To the South Coast via Galle

Day 8 brings you to Sri Lanka’s scenic southern coast. Your first stop is the colonial city of Galle, where you can explore the iconic Galle Fort and its lighthouse. Continue to a Turtle Hatchery to learn about marine conservation efforts. Finally, relax on the sandy shores of Hikkaduwa or Bentota. Spend the evening by the beach, enjoying the ocean breeze and a peaceful overnight stay at a deluxe beach resort.

Travel Distance : 180 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Overnight at : Galle

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

9Day 09: Beach Day in the South Coast

This day is yours to unwind. Soak up the sun on golden beaches shaded by swaying palms, swim in the turquoise waters, or sip a tropical cocktail while gazing at the ocean. For the more adventurous, optional activities like snorkeling or surfing are available. Relish freshly caught seafood at a seaside restaurant and enjoy your final full day in Sri Lanka. Overnight at a beachside resort in Hikkaduwa or Bentota.

Travel Distance : 50 Km

Travel Hour : 2 Hours

Overnight at : Hikkaduwa / Bentota

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)

10Day 10: Colombo City Tour & Departure

After breakfast, head to Colombo for a guided city tour. Visit colonial-era landmarks, bustling markets, serene temples, and modern shopping centers. Enjoy a final Sri Lankan meal at a renowned restaurant. Afterward, your driver will transfer you to Colombo International Airport or your hotel in Colombo/Negombo, marking the end of your memorable journey through the wonders of Sri Lanka.

Travel Distance : 120 Km

Travel Hour : 5 Hours

Drop at : Colombo / Airport

Transfer Mode : Private Vehicle (A/C)
