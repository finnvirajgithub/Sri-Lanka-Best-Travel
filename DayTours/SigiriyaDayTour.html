Sigiriya Day Tour

Home

Round Tours

"Climb, explore, taste, connect, safari"

Dive deep into Sri Lanka’s heart in a single day. Climb the majestic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, explore rural village life, visit the sacred Dambulla Cave Temple, and encounter wild elephants on a national park safari. This experience blends ancient history, natural beauty, and vibrant local culture all in one unforgettable journey.

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

1Early Departure from Colombo
Set off on an exciting day from Colombo to Sigiriya, filled with history, culture, and adventure.
2Stop 1: Sigiriya Lion Rock
Climb the legendary Lion Rock Fortress. Discover ancient frescoes, water gardens, and breathtaking views from this UNESCO World Heritage Site.
3Lunch Break
Enjoy a traditional Sri Lankan lunch at a scenic local restaurant.
4Stop 2: Traditional Village Experience
Hop on a bullock cart, paddle through village lakes, and take part in local cooking—an immersive dive into rural life.
5Stop 3: Dambulla Cave Temple
Wander through sacred caves adorned with ancient Buddhist murals and statues, telling stories over 2,000 years old.
6Stop 4: National Park Safari
End your day with a thrilling safari at Minneriya, Kaudulla, or Eco Park. Witness elephants in their natural habitat, along with rich birdlife and wildlife.
7Return to Colombo
Relax and reflect during your return journey.
