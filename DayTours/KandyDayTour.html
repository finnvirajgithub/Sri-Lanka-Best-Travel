Round Tours

"Spiritual, scenic, cultural, lively, wild"

Journey from Colombo to the cultural capital of Kandy, with a heartwarming stop at the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Discover sacred temples, traditional dance, artisan crafts, and gemstone wonders. This day tour offers a rich blend of nature, history, and Sri Lankan heritage—perfect for culture lovers and curious explorers alike.

Book Now

Tour Itinerary

1Early Departure from Colombo
Begin your cultural journey from Colombo to the lush hill country.
2Stop 1: Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
Get up close with rescued elephants. Watch them bathe and play in the river—a magical experience for animal lovers.
3Stop 2: Temple of the Tooth Relic
Visit the revered Dalada Maligawa, housing the sacred tooth of Buddha. A spiritual UNESCO-listed site filled with ritual and beauty.
4Lunch in Kandy
Savor a delicious meal at a local restaurant, choosing from traditional flavors to quick local favorites.
5Stop 3: Gem Museum
Uncover the secrets of Sri Lanka’s famed sapphires and gemstones. Learn the history of the island’s gem trade.
6Stop 4: Wood Carvings & Silk Factory
Witness skilled artisans create beautiful woodwork and handwoven silks, keeping ancient crafts alive.
7Stop 5: Traditional Cultural Dance Show
Enjoy an energetic cultural performance showcasing drumming, costumes, and traditional Kandyan dance.
